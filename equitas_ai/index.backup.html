Welcome to Equitas AI
An autonomous bias detection and remediation platform powered by Gemini AI agents. Here's how it works:
1. Upload a dataset
Drop any CSV — hiring, lending, healthcare, criminal justice. Or pick a sample dataset to start instantly.
2. Agents audit autonomously
5 Gemini AI agents run in a LangGraph loop — profiling, detecting bias, explaining why, fixing it, then re-auditing until fair.
3. Review results
See fairness metrics, regulatory violations, SHAP explanations, intersectional heatmaps, and a before/after remediation comparison.
4. Export & ask FairBot
Download a styled PDF report, generate a Google Model Card, simulate what-if scenarios, and ask FairBot any question about your audit.
